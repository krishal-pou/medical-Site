About Chowkbazar Medical

Our Story

Chowkbazar Medical is a reputable medical store located at Bharatpur-06, Chitwan, Nepal. We have been serving the local community with genuine, high-quality pharmaceutical products and health essentials.

Our store is built on the pillars of trust, transparency, and commitment to public health. We work only with licensed suppliers and ensure every product on our shelves meets the highest quality standards set by the Department of Drug Administration (DDA), Nepal.

From prescription medicines to over-the-counter products, baby care, cosmetics, and more — we are your one-stop medical shop in Chitwan.
